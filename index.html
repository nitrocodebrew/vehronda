Common Inquiries

What should I expect during my first Somatic session?

Our initial consultations are 90 minutes. We begin with a neurological assessment to understand your unique patterns of tension before moving into gentle, intentional structural work.

Do you accept insurance for Acupuncture?

While we are an out-of-network provider, we provide detailed superbills that you can submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. We also accept HSA and FSA payments.

How do the modalities work together?

Vehronda operates as a collective. Our practitioners collaborate on "Integrated Protocols" that might combine structural bodywork with nutritional support to address health from multiple angles.

Is there a cancellation policy?

We value the time of our practitioners and our clients. We require 24 hours' notice for cancellations to avoid a full session fee, allowing us to offer that space to others on our waitlist.
